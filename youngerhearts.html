Younger Hearts Program

Service Design Graphic Design UX Design Branding

As a designer from the 2016 Summer Designership, we worked with Heart & Stroke Foundation to explore and develop a health and wellness program to reduce for older adults. Younger hearts is a health and wellness program that helps older adults make small, easy changes to eat healthy and be physically active. Using Human-Centered Design we were able to bring in the voice of older adults to ensure health and wellness programs fit within their needs. I prototyped and designed concepts for the experience of specific touchpoints for onboarding participants into the health and wellness program.
